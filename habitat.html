Where Do Koalas Live?

Koalas are native to the eucalyptus forests of eastern Australia. They prefer open woodlands with plenty of eucalyptus trees, as these trees provide both food and shelter.

Koalas spend most of their lives in trees, using their sharp claws and strong limbs to climb and grip branches. They rarely come down to the ground, as they feel safest in their treetop homes.
