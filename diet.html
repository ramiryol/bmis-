What Do Koalas Eat?

Koalas primarily eat eucalyptus leaves. These leaves make up almost their entire diet, providing both food and hydration. While there are over 700 species of eucalyptus trees, koalas typically feed on just a few varieties.

Koalas are selective feeders and can consume up to 1 kg (2.2 lbs) of eucalyptus leaves a day. Their digestive system is specially adapted to process the toxins in the leaves, which would be harmful to most other animals.
